Project Biography

This project focused on designing the structural mounting system for the accumulator in the UCalgary Formula SAE electric vehicle. The design needed to provide secure mechanical support while also accounting for electrical safety, accessibility, and packaging within the vehicle.

My work involved CAD modelling the mounts, considering how the structure would interact with the chassis and surrounding components, and preparing the design for downstream manufacturing and integration. Because the accumulator is a critical subsystem, the design required careful attention to both functionality and compliance.

The project helped strengthen my skills in structural thinking, design communication, and designing within real engineering constraints. It also reinforced the importance of balancing technical performance with practical manufacturability and system-level integration.
